# Focus Management Questions Answers
## Q1: What is Deep Work?
**Deep work** is the ability to focus completely on a mentally demanding task without distractions. It helps improve productivity, learning, and the quality of work by allowing you to perform at your best.

## Q2: According to author how to do deep work properly, in a few points?
- Work on important tasks without distractions by setting dedicated focus time.
- Build a daily routine for deep work and schedule specific times for breaks or distractions.
- End each day with a shutdown ritual and plan the next day's tasks to stay focused and refreshed.


## Q3: How can you implement the principles in your day to day life?
- I will set aside dedicated, distraction-free time each day for important tasks like coding and studying.
- I will avoid unnecessary phone and social media use during focus sessions and plan my work in advance with a daily shutdown routine.


## Q4: What are the dangers of social media, in brief?
- Social media can reduce focus and productivity by causing constant distractions.
- Excessive use can increase stress, anxiety, and unhealthy comparisons with others.
- It can become addictive and reduce the time spent on learning, work, and real-life interactions.
