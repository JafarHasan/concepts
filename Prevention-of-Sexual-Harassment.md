# Prevention of Sexual Harassment
## Q1: What kinds of behaviour cause sexual harassment?
### Sexual harassment is any **unwelcome sexual behavior** including:

- Sexual jokes.
- Unwanted touching, hugging, kissing.
- Displaying sexually explicit images or videos.
- Making obscene gestures.

## Q2: What would you do in case you face or witness any incident or repeated incidents of such behaviour?

1. Clearly state that the behavior is unwelcome if it is safe to do so.
2. Document what happened, including dates, messages, and witnesses.
3. Report it through workplace, school, or organizational channels if appropriate.
4. Seek support from trusted people, HR, counselors, or local resources.
